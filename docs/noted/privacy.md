# Privacy Policy for Noted

*Last updated: February 7, 2026*

## Overview

Noted ("the app") is a voice memo and transcription app developed by Yaron Schwimmer. Your privacy is important — Noted is designed to keep all your data on your device.

## Data Collection

Noted does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Specifically:

- **Voice recordings** are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared with any third party.
- **Transcribed text** is generated on-device using Apple's built-in speech recognition and is never sent to any external server.
- **No analytics, tracking, or advertising** frameworks are included in the app.
- **No account or sign-up** is required to use the app.

## On-Device Processing

All audio recording and speech-to-text transcription is performed entirely on your device using Apple's Speech framework. No audio or text data leaves your device through the app.

## Apple Watch

When you record a voice memo on Apple Watch, the audio is transferred directly to your paired iPhone over a private connection using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. The audio is then transcribed locally on your iPhone. No data passes through any external server during this process.

## Third-Party Services

Noted does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. The app relies solely on Apple's built-in frameworks.

## Data Storage

All notes, recordings, and settings are stored in the app's private sandbox on your device. Deleting a note permanently removes its text and audio data. Uninstalling the app removes all data.
